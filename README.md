## Project Introduction
Currently, City of Philadelphia lack of clear and easy to understand data to make transportation decision, including crossing walk distance, intersection crossing signilization type. Many exsisting transportation engineer data is severlly lag behind. Updated those information would be critical for OTIS to adjust the current transportation infrastructure investment focus, like signilization changes

### Goals and Objective
Our product is in between the engineering survey and existing data. Engineering level survey (like LiDAR survey machine) only came in when major infrastructure upgrade to compliance with federal and state law and policy. The City don't have dedicated survey team. It's always costy and have a high bar.

Our product don't seek to replace survey, but provide additional information to supplement microscale decision making, like signilization priority.
